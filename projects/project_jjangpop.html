SERVICE

IOS Mobile App

Description:

My duo case partner and I created a concept for an app called COVIDiary, designed for Long COVID patients recovering from long-term COVID-19 symptoms. The app allows users to monitor their symptoms, track medication intake, and access up-to-date information, including news and recommended physical exercises. We created the concept and prototype in Figma, and developed the core functionalities into an iOS app using Swift and SwiftUI.


Tools Used:

  • XCode (Swift & SwiftUI)
  • Figma

Goal:

To create a supportive community and help people manage their long-term symptoms, combining both practical guidance and emotional support.
